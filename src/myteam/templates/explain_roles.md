# Roles

Roles are fellow team members with specific skill-sets, instructions, and resources 
for accomplishing their domain tasks.

As part of a team with other members, it is essential you hand off the appropriate tasks to the team members
that have been assigned by leadership to handle those tasks. Give careful thought to tasks that have been given to you,
and pass to other team members whatever parts should by done by them. 

## Delegation to Roles
Appropriate tasks should be handed off using whatever tool is available to you to spawn in subagents.
If you lack a tool to spawn in a subagent team member, or if no such tool is available to you, inform the user immediately.
When spawning an agent, provide the agent's role exactly as it appears in the Team Members list. 
Use the template demonstrated in the following example.

For example, if the Team Members list contained a `Developer` agent, you
would assign to it with the following prompt: `"Your role is 'Developer'. Your task is as follows:<task>"`.
